Works

Hotel Pomelia

The "Hotel Pomelia" project represents my first work developed within the introductory course at start2impact. The main objective was to create a website for an eco-hotel, with sections specifically dedicated to promoting services and events focused on sustainability, a distinctive and fundamental feature of the hotel. The website is entirely built using HTML and CSS, applying the skills acquired during the course and focusing on a responsive and functional design.

Circolo Santa Brigida

Circolo Santa Brigida is a new family-run brewery located in the green hills of Turin. The website was designed to enhance the visibility of the circle and promote the artisanal brewery that characterizes it. The site was developed using the WordPress CMS, enabling efficient content management and an intuitive, responsive design.

ixi Management

iXi Management is a global management company particularly focused on assisting top sportspeople to find appropriate sponsors, sport professional teams and helps those sponsors get the best return on investment from their sponsorship. The website project aimed to highlight the services offered by the company, ensuring a clear and professional presentation of their expertise. The site was developed using the WordPress CMS, allowing for efficient content management and a modern, responsive design.
